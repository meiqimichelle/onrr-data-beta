ONRR Data

The ONRR is responsible for management of all revenues associated with both federal offshore and onshore mineral leases. The effort is one of the federal government's greatest sources of non-tax revenues.

ONRR is the federal government's mineral revenues accountant.

ONRR collects, tracks and distributes revenue earned from mineral leases on Federal, Indian and some State land. Types of revenue include:

  • Royalties: .
  • Rents: .
  • Bonuses: .
  • Other revenues: .
After collection, these revenues are disbursed as required by contract and law to American Indian Tribes and allottees; States and their counties, parishes, and boroughs; Federal agencies; various special purpose funds; and the U.S. Treasury.
